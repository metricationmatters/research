Use Metric v2: It's Easier by 1000
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1000 millometers = 1 meter (m) [Length Base Unit]
1000 meters = 1 kilameter (Km)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1000 milloquads = 1 quad (quad) [Area Unit] = 1 square meter (m^2)
1000 quads = 1 kilaquad (Kquad)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1000 microstere = 1 millostere (mst) = 1 liter (L)
1000 millosteres = 1 stere (st) [Volume Unit] = 1 cubic meter (m^3)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
1000 milloklugs (grams) = 1 klug (kg) [Mass Base Unit]
1000 klugs = 1 kilaklug (Kkg)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------
